Accrediting Council for Independent Colleges & Schools

Advancing Educational Excellence

Founded in 1912, ACICS is a national accrediting organization of primarily degree-granting institutions.

We accredit institutions that offer programs in professional, technical, and occupational fields. ACICS is recognized by the U.S. Department of Education (ED). ACICS' scope of accreditation is recognized through the Master's Degree level.
